about me

Jeandre is a all round nerd
who loves anime, sci fi and fantasy content, and loves dnd
but just who is the weirdo?

Greetings humans. My names Jeandre and as the title says I'm a desinger/all round nerd.

I enjoy watching anime as most weebs do. Yes, I'm not ashamed to say I'm a weeb. I love gaming, focusing mainly on fantasy and sci fi. I got into designing because I have loved sketching, ever since I can remember. Drawing was a big part of my life and I think when I discovered the digital design space things just expanded from there. So I wanted to further improve my design skills by immersing myself in the adobe suite and from there finishing a three year multimedia design course. I am kind, persistant and prefer helping those around me instead of stepping over them. I believe our world has enough people willing to sacrifice their fellow man or women to get ahead in life. It's time for nice guys like me to change that. Hope you enjoy my designs and projects and if there are any queries or something I can do for you as a designer please don't hesitate to contact me.
